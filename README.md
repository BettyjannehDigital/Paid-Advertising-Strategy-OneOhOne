 Paid Advertising Strategy – OneOhOne Jewelry

**Assignment | Noroff Digital Marketing | 2026**  
**Brand:** OneOhOne Jewelry | **Platform:** Meta Ads (Instagram & Facebook)

---

## Overview

This document outlines a paid advertising campaign strategy developed for OneOhOne Jewelry. The campaign was designed to increase website traffic and brand awareness through Meta Ads, targeting women in Norway with an interest in handmade jewelry, African culture, and self-expression.

## Campaign Details

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Objective** | Website traffic & brand awareness |
| **Platform** | Instagram & Facebook (Meta Ads) |
| **Budget** | 700 NOK total (100 NOK/day × 7 days) |
| **Target Audience** | Women 18–35, Norway, interests in jewelry, African culture, handmade goods |
| **Ad Format** | Static lifestyle image with CTA |
| **Landing Page** | OneOhOne Jewelry homepage (SEO optimised) |
| **CTA** | Shop Now |

---

## Key Skills Demonstrated

- **Paid Media Strategy** — Campaign objective setting, platform selection rationale, budget allocation
- **Audience Targeting** — Demographic, geographic, and interest-based targeting via Meta Ads Manager
- **Ad Creative Briefing** — Headline, primary text, visual direction, and CTA aligned to brand tone
- **KPI Planning** — Defined success metrics: link clicks, CTR, CPC, impressions, and reach
- **Campaign Thinking** — Connecting paid strategy to overall brand goals and organic content

---

## KPIs

- Link clicks to website
- Click-Through Rate (CTR)
- Cost Per Click (CPC)
- Impressions and reach
